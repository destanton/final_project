The Application:

Seekers is designed to bring adopted people together with their birth relatives. It is a project Danielle Stanton and Tommy Huynh created to meet their final project requirements for the Backend Engineering course at The Iron Yard in Greenville, SC.

The Feature Set:

Logged in users can:

Update their profile information and set a custom photo
Fill out an "About Me" and "Biography" form
Update their profile information and set a custom photo
This information is used and filtered through in the search
Upload photos to their profile page
Search the site for other users based on multiple criteria
Send other users private messages
Import 23andMe Relative information
Imported results are stored on the user's profile page
This information can be helpful as people search a user's profile

The Roadmap:

We accomplished many key features within our limited development window. However, given this time constraint, we would like to continue working on this application with these additional features in mind:

A picture gallery for user image uploads
More "About Me" categories catering to other family members
Facebook and Google OAuth
Inclusion of search faceting

Please contact us there's something you'd like to see included as well.
