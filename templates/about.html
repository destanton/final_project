The Application:

Seekers is designed to bring adopted people together with their birth relatives. It is a project Danielle Stanton and Tommy Huynh created to meet their final project requirements for the Backend Engineering course at The Iron Yard in Greenville, SC.

The Feature Set:

Logged in users can:

  1. Update their profile information and set a custom photo
  2. Fill out an "About Me" and "Biography" form
    1. This information is used and filtered through in the search
  3. Upload photos to their profile page
  4. Search the site for other users based on multiple criteria
  5. Send other users private messages
  6. Import 23andMe Relative information
    1. Imported results are stored on the user's profile page
    2. This information can be helpful as people search a user's profile
