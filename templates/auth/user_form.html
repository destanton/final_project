Getting Started

Our application is designed to help adopted people find and reconnect with their family members.   Once you've created a user account you'll be able to:

  • Update your Profile
  • Fill out your "About Me" questions and biography. [This information is crucial as it's used in the search]
  • Send other users private messages
  • Import pictures to your profile-these will be visible to other users
  • Import your 23andMe Relative information
